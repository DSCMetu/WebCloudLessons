### Box model

- Margin  
margin is the distance between elements's borders

		margin-left: 10px;
		margin-right: 10px;
		margin-bottom: 10px;
		margin-top: 10px;
		margin: 10px;

- Padding  
padding is the distance between element and border  

		padding-left: 10px;
		padding-right: 10px;
		padding-bottom: 10px;
		padding-top: 10px;
		padding: 10px;

- height-width

		height: 100px;
		height: auto;
		height: 20%;
		width: 100px;
		width: auto;
		width: 20%;
